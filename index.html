The Lesson of Humility

A story of pride, strength, and the quiet power of humility.

Pride in Heaven

In the radiant realm of Vaikuntha, Garuda believed none could match his speed, and the Sudarshan Chakra believed nothing could resist its power. Pride shimmered in their hearts.

The Divine Plan

Vishnu observed silently. With compassion and wisdom, He decided it was time to teach a gentle but unforgettable lesson.

Hanuman Arrives

Hanuman, calm and devoted, stood with folded hands. Though capable of unimaginable strength, he carried no pride within him.

The Test

Garuda flew at lightning speed—but Hanuman arrived first. The Sudarshan Chakra spun fiercely—but Hanuman stopped it with effortless calm.

The Realization

Garuda lowered his head. The Chakra dimmed its glow. They understood: power without humility loses its shine.

True strength is not loud. True power does not boast. Stay humble, and you will always grow brighter.
